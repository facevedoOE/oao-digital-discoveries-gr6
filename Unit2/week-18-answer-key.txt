───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q1. From Unit 1: Which of the following is the BEST example
    of ASYNCHRONOUS communication?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  A) A live Zoom class with 20 students
     ❌ Zoom is synchronous — everyone is present at the
        same time.
  B) An email sent Tuesday night, read Wednesday morning   ✓ CORRECT
     ✅ Email is classic asynchronous communication —
        sender and receiver are not online at the same
        time. (SC.6.CC.1.1)
  C) A FaceTime call with your grandparent
     ❌ FaceTime is synchronous — both people are on the
        call together.
  D) A voice chat during an online game
     ❌ Voice chat is synchronous — you both speak in real
        time.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q2. From Unit 2: A photo file is sitting on your phone,
    untouched in your camera roll. What state is that data
    in?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  A) Data in motion
     ❌ Data in motion is traveling across a network. The
        photo isn't moving.
  B) Data at rest                                          ✓ CORRECT
     ✅ Data at rest means stored on a device and not
        actively moving or being processed. (SC.6.CS.1.1)
  C) Data in use
     ❌ Data in use means open in an app and being read or
        processed.
  D) Data in transit
     ❌ "In transit" is another way to describe data in
        motion — but the photo isn't moving.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q3. From Unit 2: Which of these should be classified as
    CONFIDENTIAL information — never shared online, no
    matter who claims to ask?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  A) Your favorite music genre
     ❌ Music tastes are PUBLIC — fine to share.
  B) The name of your school
     ❌ A school name is generally PUBLIC — printed on
        jerseys and websites.
  C) Your password and the 6-digit MFA code that just
     texted to your phone                                   ✓ CORRECT
     ✅ Passwords and MFA codes are CONFIDENTIAL. Real
        companies will NEVER ask for them.
        (SC.6.CS.1.4)
  D) Your favorite NBA team
     ❌ Sports preferences are PUBLIC.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q4. From Unit 2: What is LATERAL READING?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  A) Reading the same article over and over until you
     remember it
     ❌ Re-reading the same page doesn't fact-check it.
  B) Leaving the page to fact-check it on other trusted
     sources                                                ✓ CORRECT
     ✅ Lateral reading means leaving the page and
        searching what other trusted sources say.
        (SC.6.HS.1.2)
  C) Reading by holding the screen sideways
     ❌ Lateral reading isn't about screen orientation.
  D) Skipping every other paragraph to save time
     ❌ That's skim-reading. Lateral reading means cross-
        checking with OTHER sources.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q5. What does METACOGNITION mean?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  A) Memorizing as many vocabulary words as possible
     ❌ Memorizing facts is just memorizing — not
        metacognition.
  B) Thinking about your own thinking — what you know,
     how you learned it, what's still unclear              ✓ CORRECT
     ✅ Metacognition is the reflective thinking about
        your own learning that powers real growth.
        (ELA.K12.EE.5.1)
  C) Cheating on assignments to get a higher grade
     ❌ Definitely not. Metacognition is honest
        reflection on YOUR learning.
  D) Watching educational videos at 2x speed
     ❌ Speed has nothing to do with metacognition. The
        point is THINKING about your thinking.
