What Is Digital Communication? — Reflection
Digital Discoveries  ·  Course #0200021  ·  Grade 6
💬Your Prompt
Think about the digital communication tools you use most often — at home, at school, or for fun. Choose ONE tool and describe how you use it. Then explain: Is your communication with that tool usually synchronous or asynchronous? How does that timing affect how you write your messages? Would you communicate differently if you were using a different tool? Use specific examples from your own experience.
💡 Tips for a Strong Response
  • Name a specific tool (e.g., Canvas Inbox, Discord, text messaging, ENGAGE VR).
  • Use the words synchronous or asynchronous correctly in your post.
  • Give at least one concrete example from your own life — not a generic answer.
  • After posting, reply to one classmate who chose a different tool than you did.
Standard: SC.6.CC.1.1  ·  Minimum: 4–6 sentences  ·  Reply to at least 1 classmate
